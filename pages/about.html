About Turbo and Electric Sales and Service


Turbo and Electric Sales and Service (Formerly Copperstate Sales & Service) was founded in 1969 by D.R. "Doc" Schoenberger after he worked for several years at Menke Transportation Company in Phoenix. Air Research asked Doc to coordinate a test program to increase the power of their over-the-road long haul trucks. Performing successfully for them, Doc opened Copperstate Sales & Service, which would become Turbo and Electric in 1972. Since then, his daughter and son-in-law, the Fargotsteins, have continued his quality and service tradition.

Turbo & Electric Sales & Service sells and services Alternators, Starters and Turbochargers, including pre-WWII varieties, classic cars, agricultural, fire operations, law enforcement, mass transit, heavy duty construction equipment, mining, RV and marine. We can also service various 12 & 24 volt motors and fan clutches. We have served automotive fleets and customers all throughout the southwestern United States. We offer Nationwide shipping, drop shipping, local pick-up and delivery service.
