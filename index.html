Services


We sell and service Alternators, Starters, Turbochargers and Fan Clutch Assemblies. We also perform DC electric motor repair. We have availability of Brand New aftermarket units, OEM Direct replacement units, & premium quality remanufactured units. If your unit is hard to find, we can rebuild all makes and models. Our rebuilt units will give you years of dependable service. We stand behind all products that we sell. Don't waste your time going to the chain stores or ordering online. Call or bring your old unit in for a quote today. We also offer pick-up and delivery service to qualified customers. Charge accounts available. Come in and fill out a credit application to qualify.


We supply high quality new OEM, aftermarket and rebuilt alternators. If we don't have your particular unit in stock, we can rebuild yours. We carry Delco 50DN's, EMP 450's, and Leece Neville OEM units.
